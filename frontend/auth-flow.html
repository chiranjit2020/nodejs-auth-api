Why you see 2 requests for /refresh:

This is normal! Preflight happens because:

  • Frontend (localhost:5500) ‚â† Backend (localhost:5000) = Cross-origin
  • Using Content-Type: application/json

üéØ Key Concepts Summary

Access Token: Your "entry pass" - expires quickly (1min) for security
Refresh Token: Your "renewal pass" - lasts longer (7d), used to get new entry passes
Why 2 tokens? Security! If someone steals your access token, it only works for 1 minute. The refresh token is rarely sent over the network.
Automatic refresh: Your frontend handles token refresh automatically. Users never know their token expired!
